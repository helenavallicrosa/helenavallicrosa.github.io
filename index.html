Terrestrial ecologist interested in plant elemental composition and stoichiometry, biogeochemical cycles, plant-soil interactions, and how those interact with the carbon cycle and global change. I have worked in Mediterranean and Tropical ecosystems but I have also worked at regional and global scales by using computational science, data synthesis, and artificial intelligence algorithms.

I have an interest in teaching, and I enjoy sharing science with the general public, making it reachable and understandable to different ages and backgrounds.
